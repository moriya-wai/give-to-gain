This is a curated recognition opportunity for women who have given with AI through a real initiative—such as a community program, research contribution, open resource, ethical AI leadership, or an AI-powered product/project with measurable benefit.

To be eligible, nominees must:
  • Have led or significantly contributed to an AI initiative (project, product, research, resource, program, or deployment)
  • Be able to explain the problem, approach, and impact
  • Be willing to deliver a 5–7 minute spotlight with 3–6 slides at Give to Gain: IWD 2026 — Halifax
Note: This is not a promotional pitch. If your initiative is commercial, the focus must be on what you gave and what others gained.
